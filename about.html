About Me

Ever since I was a child, I've had an insatiable curiosity about how things work, whether they belong to the tech world or beyond. This curiosity has driven me to explore and learn about a wide array of domains.

Alongside web development, I've been nurturing my skills in Machine Learning, fascinated by its potential to revolutionize industries and improve lives. Competitive Programming has also been a significant part of my journey, pushing me to think critically and solve complex problems efficiently.

I am open to opportunities as a developer, eager to gain valuable experience that will help me hone my skills further. I look forward to expanding my network and connecting with like-minded individuals. Whether it's through collaborations, communities, or professional networks, I believe that sharing knowledge and experiences with others is immensely valuable.
